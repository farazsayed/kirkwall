Kirkwall Capital is an investment platform focused on providing superior risk-adjusted returns to its investors through the acquisition and active management of commercial real estate assets within strategically selected markets.


Strategy

Kirkwall Capital implements a fundamental value approach to investing, seeking to acquire assets below intrinsic value and below replacement cost and enhance asset value through strategic capital improvements, repositioning and active management.

Kirkwall Capital targets non-institutionally managed assets in strategic markets that exhibit strong growth fundamentals, providing existing cash flow and the opportunity to enhance value for future capital appreciation.

Team

The firm’s partners collectively bring 15 years of experience and have closed over $5 billion in transactions, bringing an institutional mindset and investment approach to a traditionally non-institutional space within commercial real estate.

Faiz Sayed

Managing Partner

Prior to co-founding Kirkwall Capital, Faiz was a Vice President at TPG Capital in their Real Estate Finance group in San Francisco where he focused on commercial real estate debt investing in the West Coast and Texas markets. At TPG he was responsible for underwriting, structuring, and originating large mezzanine and first mortgage loans for properties undergoing value-creation processes including re- leasing, repositioning, and construction. Prior to TPG, Faiz was at Wells Fargo where he was an Associate in their Commercial Real Estate Group in Dallas and a graduate of their Credit Management Training Program in San Francisco.

Faiz has closed over $4 billion of commercial real estate transactions throughout his career.

Faiz has bachelor’s degrees in finance and economics from Southern Methodist University and is an Associate Board Member of the Folsom Institute for Real Estate at the SMU Cox School of Business.
